Poste

Poste is an app that will allow to pull in all the saved posts from social media into one place.

Currently most of use at least 3 social media platforms. Social media gives us really great content about the some good ideas, new restaurants and travel ideas.

Many times though we forget where the saved items are or it gets lost with all the saved ones. Poste will allow to bring all your saved items into one app from where it will suggest to allocate the apps in different folders or you can organize them yourself in logical folders which you can then share with your friends and you can all collaborate in a folder!

Use Case:scrolling through social media platforms such as Facebook, Instagram and Tik Tok then saving it in the folder.

Poste app will pull from the saved folders across all these social media platforms and it will create folders based on the tags and then it will be shareable with others.
